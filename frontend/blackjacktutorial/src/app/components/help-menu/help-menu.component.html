How to Play Blackjack

Blackjack is a popular card game that is played in casinos worldwide. The goal of the game is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21 points.

Here are the basic rules of Blackjack:

  • Each player is dealt two cards.
  • Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value.
  • Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are worth 10 points each.
  • An Ace can be worth either 1 point or 11 points, depending on which value is more favorable for the hand.
  • The player can choose to "Hit" to receive additional cards or "Stand" to keep their current hand.
  • If the player's hand exceeds 21 points, they "Bust" and lose the round.
  • After the player's turn, the dealer reveals their second card and follows specific rules for drawing cards.
  • The dealer must draw cards until they have a hand value of at least 17.
  • If the dealer's hand exceeds 21 points, they "Bust," and all remaining players win.
  • If the dealer does not bust, the hands are compared, and the closest hand to 21 points wins.
  • If the player's hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's hand, the player wins and receives a payout.
  • If the dealer's hand is closer to 21 or the player busts, the dealer wins.

Remember, the key to Blackjack is to get as close to 21 points without going over. Good luck and have fun!
